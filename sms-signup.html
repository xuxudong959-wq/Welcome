Patient Text Message Sign-Up

Welcome Family Medicine, PA is a family medicine clinic at 9160 Bellaire Blvd, Suite E, Houston, TX 77036 serving Houston's Chinatown community. We send a small number of text messages each month to remind patients about appointments and ask them to call us when the doctor needs them to come back in. This page lets current and prospective patients confirm they want to receive those reminders.

This is a real opt-in. Submitting this form is the same as checking the SMS reminder box on the paper registration form you fill out at the clinic. Clinic staff review and record every sign-up before activating reminders. You can stop at any time by replying STOP to any of our messages.

What you will receive

  • Appointment reminders and confirmations
  • Care follow-up messages asking you to call the clinic
  • Important practice notices (closures, holidays, weather)

Typically a few messages per month. Message and data rates may apply. Consent is not a condition of receiving care. Mobile information is never shared with third parties for marketing.

Prefer to sign up in person or by phone?

You don't need to use this form. You can also opt in to text reminders by:

  • In person at the clinic: Check the "I would like to receive text message reminders" box on the paper patient registration form at the front desk and sign next to it. Clinic staff date and initial your sign-up in the chart.
  • By phone: Call (713) 995-8886 during business hours and tell our staff you'd like to receive text reminders. Staff will verify your identity and record your sign-up in the chart with date and initials.
  • By text: If you've previously opted out, reply START to any of our previous messages to re-subscribe.
HIPAA & privacy: The clinic does not send protected health information (PHI) in text messages. Lab results, diagnoses, and treatment details are handled by phone or in person. Texts are limited to reminders and a request to call back.
