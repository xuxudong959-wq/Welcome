About

Welcome Family Medicine, PA is a family medicine practice serving the Houston Chinatown community and surrounding neighborhoods. We provide comprehensive primary care for adults and seniors in a multilingual environment (Mandarin, Cantonese, English, and Spanish).

Our team includes Dr. Xudong Xu and Dr. Yongfang Chen. We offer routine checkups, chronic disease management (diabetes, hypertension, etc.), preventive care, vaccinations, lab work, and care coordination.

About Family Medicine

Family medicine doctors, also known as primary care physicians (PCPs), are trained to meet the basic health needs of children and adults. They often refer patients to specialists or for further testing, and they treat common conditions such as diabetes, high cholesterol, and allergies. They also perform physicals, give vaccines, and may do some in-office lab testing.
